Houses

Gryffindor

Gryffindor values courage and bravery. The house was founded by Godric Gryffindor.

  • Key Traits:
    • Bravery
    • Courage
    • Determination
  • Famous Members:
    1. Harry Potter
    2. Hermione Granger
    3. Ron Weasley

Slytherin

Slytherin values ambition and resourcefulness. The house was founded by Salazar Slytherin.

  • Key Traits:
    • Ambition
    • Leadership
    • Cunning
  • Famous Members:
    1. Draco Malfoy
    2. Severus Snape
    3. Tom Riddle (Lord Voldemort)

Ravenclaw

Ravenclaw values intelligence and wisdom. The house was founded by Rowena Ravenclaw.

  • Key Traits:
    • Intelligence
    • Creativity
    • Originality
  • Famous Members:
    1. Luna Lovegood
    2. Cho Chang
    3. Filius Flitwick

Hufflepuff

Hufflepuff values loyalty and hard work. The house was founded by Helga Hufflepuff.

  • Key Traits:
    • Loyalty
    • Patience
    • Dedication
  • Famous Members:
    1. Cedric Diggory
    2. Nymphadora Tonks
    3. Pomona Sprout
